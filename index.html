About me

I am an entrepreneur with a background in technology and a passion for ambitious internet startups. Having started my first business at the mature age of 15 and ever since felt like I found the true love of my life - apart from my lovely supportive family - I feel very connected to entrepreneurship.

I was born in Finland but have spent significant time abroad in the UK, Germany, Switzerland and the Netherlands. Currently I'm chasing my next big venture out of the small Maltese islands in the middle of the Mediterranean Sea.

So far I've exited succesfully from 3 of my past 6 ventures with the latest being Maventa™ - which was acquired by Visma in Jan 2013. Maventa was founded in 2007 and grew to become one of the leading European providers in electronic invoicing with over 20,000 corporate clients at the time.

I focus mainly on companies trying to solve a specific customer problem and getting maximum reach for it. Employing guerrilla tactics and manouvering in hostile environments is something that I do naturally. Always having been the David against the Goliaths have landed me a few tricks up my sleeve over the years.

My lifestyle could be described as somewhat sporadic, impulsive and hedonistic. I do not distinguish between work and leisure - neither do I aspire to. Love for simple things in life such as good food & wine, traveling, diving and fishing combined with precious time spent around family and friends is something I cherish dearly.
